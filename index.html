About

A new dimension of
Vietnamese cinema

Founded in the 21st century, CD Singularity Films was born from a singular idea: that every story deserves to exist, regardless of cost or constraint.

We are solving the Vietnamese filmmaker's problem with science solutions — creating tools, spaces, and methodologies that liberate creativity from its material limitations.
